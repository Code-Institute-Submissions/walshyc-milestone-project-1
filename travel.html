Travel

Gibraltar

  • Stadium - Victoria Stadium
  • Capacity 2,500
  • Ticket Allocation - estimated 200
  • Opened 1971
  • Surface Astro Turf

Flights

The closest airports with direct flights from Ireland are Malaga and Seville. There is also direct flights from the UK to Gibraltar itself.

Accommodation

The two cities that are closest to the stadium are Gibraltar and La Linea across the border in Spain. There are a large number of hotels in both cities.

Copenhagen

  • Stadium - Parken Stadium
  • Capacity 38,065
  • Ticket Allocation 1,900
  • Opened 1992
  • Surface Grass

Flights

Direct flights from Dublin to Copenhagen with Ryanair, SAS & Norwegian Air.

Accommodation

Numerous hotels in copenhagen. Our group stayed in Saga Hotel last time we played Denmark in 2018.

Tbilisi

  • Stadium - Boris Paichadze Dinamo Arena
  • Capacity 54,549
  • Ticket Allocation 2,700
  • Opened 1976
  • Surface Grass

Flights

There is no direct flights from Dublin to Tbilisi. You need to transfer in Amsterdam, Paris or Istanbul.

Accommodation

Hotels in Tbilisi are very cheap and plentiful. We stayed in the Mercure hotel last time Ireland played there in 2017.

Basel

  • Stadium - St. Jacob Park
  • Capacity 37,500
  • Ticket Allocation 1,875
  • Opened 2001
  • Surface Grass

Flights

Direct flights from Dublin to Basle with Ryanair=. Swiss Air, Ryanair & Aer Lingus fly direct to Zurich, which is one hour away by train.

Accommodation

Hotels are expensive in Swiotzerland in general, so maybe AirBNB might be an option for this game.
